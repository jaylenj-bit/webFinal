Why Sales Matters

Sales helps businesses grow. If you know how to talk to people and understand what they need, you can sell better.

Basic Sales Tips

1. Be Confident

Speak clearly and believe in what you are saying.

2. Listen First

Listen to what the customer wants before trying to sell.

3. Explain the Value

Show the person why the product or service can help them.

4. Be Friendly

People are more likely to buy from someone they like and trust.

Final Advice

Good sales is not about forcing people. It is about helping them make a good decision.
